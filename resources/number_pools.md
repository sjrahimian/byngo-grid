# Byngo Cards Generator Details

A breakdown of the number pools the Byngo program will use to generate the columns, and how to win in the game.

## 3x3 Bingo (30-Ball / Speed Bingo)

Designed for quick matches this will be the fastest version of bingo you'll likely play, unless you play 2x2 or 1x1 version (not available in this program).

- Number Pool:
  - BI: 1–10
  - N: 11–20
  - GO: 21–30
- How to Win:
  - Full House (standard): Mark all 9 numbers (a.k.a. Blackout).
  - Single Lines: 3 marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

## 4x4 Bingo (80-Ball Bingo)

A comfortable middle ground between the slower 3x3 bingo and faster than 5x5 bingo.

- Number Pool:
  - Column 1: 1–20
  - Column 2: 21–40
  - Column 3: 41–60
  - Column 4: 61–80
- How to Win:
  - Single Lines (standard): 4 marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  - Four Corners: Marking the numbers in the extreme corners.
  - Square/Frame: Marking all numbers on the outer edge.
  - Full House: Mark all 16 numbers.

## 5x5 Bingo (75-Ball / Traditional Bingo)

This is the standard version of the game.

- Number Pool:
  - B: 1–15
  - I: 16–30
  - N: 31–45
  - G: 46–60
  - O: 61–75
- How to Win:
  - Single Lines (standard): 5 marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  - Blackout: Mark all 24 numbers.
  - Postage Stamp: A 2x2 square in a corner.
  - Plus Sign: Marking the entire middle row and middle column to form a "+".
